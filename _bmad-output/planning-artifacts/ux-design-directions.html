Lifeline UX Design Directions

Six ways Lifeline could feel.

These mockups explore layout, density, visual hierarchy, and interaction emphasis for a private life reflection product. Each direction uses the same foundation: warm minimalism, clear privacy cues, a vertical life-line, and pattern discovery.
