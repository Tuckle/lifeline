# Story 2.7: Future Intentions Below Present

Status: done

## Story

As an authenticated Lifeline user,  
I want to add future intentions below the present point,  
so that Lifeline connects my past memories with what I want to build next.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given I am on the Timeline or Add route, when I choose Add future intention, then I see a focused form for intention title and optional future date or approximate future period, and saving does not require reflection or pattern-linking features from future stories.
2. Given I save a valid future intention, when I return to the timeline, then the intention appears below the present point, and it is visually distinct from past memories without relying only on color.
3. Given I view a future intention, when I open its detail or actions, then I can edit or delete the intention, and destructive deletion uses clear confirmation copy.
4. Given I create or edit a future intention, when it is persisted, then it is user-owned and protected by the same private data access model as timeline events.
5. Given I view the future area on mobile or desktop, when the timeline renders, then the future direction remains understandable, and intention markers do not overlap with the present marker, cards, labels, or controls.
6. Given saving or deleting a future intention fails, when the app shows feedback, then the message is calm, specific, and recoverable, and user-entered draft text is preserved where practical.
